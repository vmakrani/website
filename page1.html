U.S. Gun Violence Analysis

Here's my Tableau Gun Violence dashboard: an insightful look into gun violence trends in the U.S. – years of data, all loaded into one visual chamber!

Key Insights:
- The most violent day in the last decade was October 1, 2017 with 62 people killed and 443 people injured by guns
- 2021 was the most violent year in the last decade with 3530 victims
- Illinois is the state with the most gun violence

Candy Store Sales Analysis

Welcome to The Candyland Chronicles! My Tableau dashboard has the scoop on U.S. candy sales, courtesy of a tasty Kaggle dataset. Sweet tooths nationwide, your data is served!

Key Insights:
- In 2024, the top selling chocolate in California was the "Wonka Bar - Triple Dazzel Caramel" with over 764 units sold
- The People of Nevada, Wyoming, and Rhode Island don't have much of a sweet tooth!
- With only 4 units sold and $6 in sales in 2024, I can confidently say that no one loves Nerds :(

Sales Analysis Dashboard

Tool: Power BI Desktop
Industry: Retail (AdventureWorks DB on SQL Server)

This dashboard shows the total sales across countries, months, category and products. The KPI cards show us the latest sales metrics for the year and their trend over the last 12 months. Clicking on the visualizations allow you to filter into specific countries, months and categories of products.
